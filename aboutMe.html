This blog will be detailing my time at Skill Distillery, and showcasing the knoweldge I gain from the boot camp. I have little experience in the topic right now, but am looking forward to learning something knew, as well as sharing my experience as it unfolds. I will (hopefully) be updating this blog a few times a week with my stories, experiences, and new projects. This is an exciting time for me, and I am very grateful to have the chance to take part in Skill Distillery's Java Boot Camp.

Thank you again for stopping by. I hope you decide to stay :)
